About our network

Our Founding

The Nebula was founded in December 2011 under this policy: The community comes first. Always. The Nebula was built around the better good of the community and the end goal that every person on every server is striving to have. Fun. From there we made a few little minigames and grew a small playerbase. After about a year and a half, The Nebula shut down.


Under Development

We're bringing The Nebula back! We are going to have numerous custom gametypes! We'll give you a sneak peak by letting you know the first two servers we will be releasing is KitPvP with Jetpacks and PaintWars in Space. We know you'll love it!
