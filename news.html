Say hello to Nebula Factions.

Say hello to Nebula Factions.

Written by IceCubeCat, September 2.



What is Nebula Factions? Factions servers. You can't get any more generic, right? Wrong. We're teaming up with exclusive teams of developers and builders to make something truly awesome- and yes, unique. We're abandoning the idea of basic factions and making it so that once you join, there are three factions you can choose from. Names will be announced soon. Each faction will have a separate spawn, which is their base. These three factions will rage war over each other to take over as much as the world as possible. This is never ending, the objective is to own the most blocks and stay that way. This is a team-building game. One person can impact the whole team's stats, and reputation.
Spawns Each spawn will be different colors, but will look relatively like this: '
More Information We are open to all recommendations to faction names. There must be three, and all 3 must be space related. Also feel free to post any comments if you want to be a faction leader. Note to Staff: Also, staff members, feel free to contact me personally on Skype about becoming the manager of Nebula Factions. The manager is in charge of the whole server, it's doings, users, and updates. You are allowed to promote an assistant. All of this must go by the Founder, myself, before being established. @Vast_Venom is the new Factions Manager! Congratulations! Please note users: Ganging up on one faction is allowed, as long as the two that are ganging on them have an established peace treaty. Peace treaties only last 48 hours, and must run by the manager of Nebula Factions before being established as official. More information will be added here. This is just a basic overview. If you watch the thread, you will get a notification when updates come out. Or, you can PM me and ask for notifications from myself for updates on Nebula Factions.
